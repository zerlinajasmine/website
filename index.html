My Hobbies

Outside of work, I find myself constantly drawn to stories—whether they're found in places, music, or the pages of a good book.

Traveling has always been one of the ways I learn about the world and, in many ways, about myself. So far, I've explored Java, Sumatra, Kalimantan, and Bali, each with its own culture, people, and unforgettable moments. There are still so many places on my wishlist, both across Indonesia and beyond, and I hope to continue collecting experiences one journey at a time.

Music is another constant in my life. I enjoy playing the piano, guitar, and violin, and each instrument offers a different way to express emotions that words sometimes can't fully capture. Whether I'm practicing a familiar melody or simply playing for myself, it's always a quiet reminder to slow down and be present.

If given the choice between a movie and a book, I'll almost always choose the book. I love how reading invites imagination—allowing every character, every place, and every emotion to come alive in a way that's uniquely personal. For me, books don't just tell stories; they leave room for me to become part of them.

Looking back, I think there's a common thread connecting everything I love: curiosity. Curiosity about new places, different cultures, meaningful conversations, and the countless stories people carry with them. It's what inspires me to keep exploring, keep learning, and keep seeing beauty in both extraordinary adventures and the quiet moments in between.

At the heart of it all, I believe everyone has a story worth telling. I'm simply someone who never gets tired of listening to them.
